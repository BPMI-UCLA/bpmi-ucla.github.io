About Us

What is BPMI?

BPMI Stands for Bruin Project Management Initiatives, a bridge connecting you with other UCLA student clubs and organizations, and also a platform providing resources from corporates and enterprises.

Our Goals

We help build bridges for student groups to connect with other student groups, individuals, and companies. We are platforms for sharing information and resources by initiating and executing various projects.
