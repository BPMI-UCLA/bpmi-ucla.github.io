Impressions COVID

Impressions COVID is an online exhibition reflecting on the pandemic. By including artworks of any form, we hope to provide a safe space for all to share their unique perspectives in this special time, hence capturing and reflecting upon the diverse experiences bestowed by the pandemic, from the US and beyond.

About Us

Who are we?

Founded in spring 2020, the Bruin Project Management Initiatives (BPMI) is UCLA’s first non-profit, student run project management organization.

Our Goals

We undertake various types of projects to provide platforms for idea sharing, conversations and bonding. Our mission is to foster communications between our community members and beyond.
